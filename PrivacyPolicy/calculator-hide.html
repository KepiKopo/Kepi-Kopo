Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 7 December 2025

1. Introduction

Calculator Hide Photos, Videos & Files (referred to as "App", "we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we keep your data secure.

2. Information We Collect

a) Personal Information

We do not collect any personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or home address.

However, if you contact us directly (for support or inquiries), we may receive your email address. This information is used only for communicating with you and resolving your request. We do not store, share, or use it for any other purpose.

b) Photos, Videos, and Files

  • The photos, videos, and files you hide inside the App are stored locally on your device.
  • Your private files are never uploaded to any server.
  • We cannot access, view, or share your hidden media files under any circumstances.

c) App Usage Data

We may collect limited technical information such as:

  • App performance data
  • Crash logs

This data is used solely to improve the stability, performance, and user experience of the App.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information only for:

  • Improving App performance
  • Detecting and fixing bugs
  • Enhancing the overall user experience

We never use, view, or share your private photos, videos, or files.

4. Storage & Security

  • All hidden files remain stored only on your device and are never transmitted to any external server.
  • Your PIN/Password is also saved locally and is not shared with any third party.

We take data security seriously and implement reasonable measures to help protect your information.

5. Third-Party Services

The App may integrate certain third-party services (such as analytics or advertising). These services may collect basic technical data like:

  • Device model
  • General App activity

These third-party services cannot access your hidden photos, videos, or files.

6. Children's Privacy

  • This App is not intended for children under the age of 13.
  • We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be displayed within the App or on our website.
